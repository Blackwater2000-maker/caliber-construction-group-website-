Questions & Answers

Florida-specific answers grounded in current Florida Building Code where it applies.

Most roofing contractors operate through layers of subcontractors and project managers who never set foot on your roof. We built a compressed, highly functional team — senior people on your job site, not behind a desk. Every project has a dedicated on-site senior team member overseeing the crew, inspecting installation, and ensuring every detail meets our standard. We also pull all permits, manage all inspections, and hand you documentation at completion. That level of direct accountability is what we mean when we say Caliber.

Yes. Caliber Construction Group holds Florida State Certified Roofing Contractor License #CCC1337709. We are fully licensed and insured for all roofing work across our 7-county service area. We pull permits on every permitted project and manage inspections through completion.

We serve Orange, Seminole, Osceola, Lake, Polk, Volusia, and Brevard counties — the full Central Florida market. If you're in the greater Orlando metro area or surrounding counties, give us a call.

Most residential replacements are completed in one day for shingle systems. Tile and metal systems typically take two to three days depending on square footage and complexity. Commercial projects vary by scope. We provide a firm schedule at time of contract and commit to it.

The My Safe Florida Home Program is administered by the Florida Department of Financial Services and provides qualifying homeowners with a free wind inspection and matching grants of up to $10,000 for hurricane-hardening improvements to their homes. Roof upgrades that improve wind resistance are among the most common qualifying uses. We can help you understand whether your project may qualify and how to navigate the program. Contact us for details.

After storm damage, the first priority is securing your roof to prevent further water intrusion. We provide quick emergency response and can tarp and secure your roof same-day. From there, we conduct a thorough damage assessment with detailed photography and written documentation. We coordinate directly with your insurance adjuster, provide our documentation package, and advocate for full coverage of the necessary restoration scope. We work with all major Florida carriers.

We offer financing options through third-party lenders, including 0% interest promotional periods for qualifying homeowners and low monthly payment plans. Most applications receive a same-day decision. When your insurance covers part of the cost and you need to finance the remainder, we coordinate both seamlessly. Contact us for current financing details and to discuss what works for your situation.
