German Engineering

The Noble Matte Difference

Worthouse stone-coated steel roofing is engineered in Germany and manufactured to standards that exceed what U.S.-spec roofing products typically require. The panel substrate is Galvalume steel — a zinc-aluminum alloy coating that provides superior corrosion resistance compared to standard galvanized steel.

The Noble Matte surface coating is a proprietary acrylic-over-stone finish that combines the UV stability of acrylic with the thermal and acoustic performance of the embedded stone chip layer. The result is a roof that is cooler, quieter, and more impact-resistant than any competing system at comparable price points.

For Florida homeowners who experience hurricane seasons, the 200 mph wind rating and FORTIFIED eligibility represent real, measurable risk reduction — not marketing language. FORTIFIED certification can qualify homeowners for insurance discounts of 20–50% or more, depending on carrier and county.
