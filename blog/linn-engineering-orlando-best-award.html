Spotlight: Linn Engineering & Design — Orlando's Best Building Engineer

Caliber Construction Group 2 min read Winter Park, FL

We want to take a moment to recognize a respected name in the Central Florida construction community — Linn Engineering & Design, Inc., based right here in Winter Park, FL.

Founded in 2006, Linn Engineering has spent nearly two decades building a reputation for structural and building engineering across Florida's commercial and residential construction industry. Their attention to detail and knowledge of Florida building code has made them a well-known and trusted firm throughout the region.

Orlando's Best — Building Engineer

Linn Engineering & Design has earned recognition as Orlando's Best Building Engineer — a community award voted on by locals across the region. A well-deserved nod to a firm that has been a trusted presence in Florida's construction industry since 2006.

A Trusted Name in Central Florida

Linn Engineering & Design operates out of 711 Executive Drive in Winter Park and has built a strong reputation across a wide range of Florida construction projects. If you're a homeowner, developer, or contractor in need of structural engineering services, they're worth a call.

At Caliber, we believe in recognizing quality work in our local industry. Linn Engineering's Orlando's Best recognition reflects the kind of standard we respect — and it's great to see firms like this acknowledged by the community they serve.
