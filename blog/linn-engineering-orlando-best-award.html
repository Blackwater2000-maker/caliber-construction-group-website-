Who Is Linn Engineering & Design?

Founded in 2006 and headquartered at 711 Executive Drive in Winter Park, FL, Linn Engineering & Design, Inc. is a full-service structural and building engineering firm that has spent nearly two decades transforming Florida's commercial and residential construction landscape. From structural assessments to complex design challenges, their team brings a depth of expertise that sets projects up for long-term success.

Their work spans a wide range of project types across Central Florida — and their reputation for accuracy, responsiveness, and professionalism is exactly why Caliber Construction Group trusts them on our projects.

Why This Partnership Matters to You

When you hire Caliber Construction Group, you're not just getting a roofing crew — you're getting the full backing of a vetted engineering team. That means when your project requires structural review, load calculations, or permitting support, we have the right professionals already in our corner.

This matters most on complex projects: commercial roofing systems, large residential re-roofs, storm damage assessments, and new construction builds where engineering sign-off is required by Florida building code. Having Linn Engineering in our network means fewer delays, faster permitting, and a finished product that's built to last.
