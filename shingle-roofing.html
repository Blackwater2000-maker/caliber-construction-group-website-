Asphalt Shingle Roofing

Expert Shingle Installation for Central Florida Homes

Built for Florida's Climate

The Right Shingle for Every Florida Home

Asphalt shingles are engineered for Florida's climate — algae-resistant coatings, high-wind ratings, and UV-stable granule layers that hold up under intense sun exposure year after year.

We install the full range of shingle systems from architectural dimensional shingles to Class 4 impact-resistant options that can qualify for homeowner's insurance premium reductions. Every installation is permitted and inspected to Florida building code.

Our dedicated on-site managers oversee every crew and every material delivery — so what gets installed is exactly what you approved, done exactly as the manufacturer specifies.

  • Architectural, dimensional, and designer shingle options
  • Class 4 impact-resistant systems — insurance discount eligible
  • Algae and mold-resistant coatings standard
  • High-wind rated — up to 130 mph
  • SBS-modified shingles available for superior flexibility
  • Permits pulled and managed — we handle all paperwork
  • Dedicated on-site manager on every job
● 5-Year Workmanship Warranty

Caliber Construction Group stands behind every installation. Our 5-year workmanship warranty covers installation defects, flashing, seaming, and fastening details — clearly documented and handed to you at project completion. Material warranties — typically 25–50 years — are provided by the manufacturer and also transferred to you at completion.
