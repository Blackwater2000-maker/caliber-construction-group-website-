Our Guarantee

Warranties & Our Guarantee

Every Caliber project is backed by our 5-year workmanship warranty plus the manufacturer warranty — clearly documented and handed to you at project completion.

5
Year Workmanship Warranty
The Caliber Workmanship Guarantee

Every installation is backed by our 5-year workmanship warranty. If a defect in our installation workmanship causes a problem within 5 years, we fix it at no charge.

  • Covers flashing, seaming, and fastening details
  • Applies to every roofing system we install
  • Transferable to new homeowners
  • Full documentation provided at completion
  • No deductibles, no exclusions on our work
25–50yr
Manufacturer Material Warranty
Material Warranties by System

Every system we install is backed by the manufacturer's material warranty, transferred to you in writing at project completion.

  • Asphalt shingles: 25–50 years
  • Stone-coated metal tile: 50 years
  • Standing seam metal: 25–40 years
  • Clay & concrete tile: Lifetime
  • TPO flat roofing: 10–20 years
