Our Story

A Different Kind of Roofing Company

Who We Are

Built on a Simple Idea

Caliber Construction Group was built on a simple idea: hire experienced craftsmen, install the best materials, and deliver a finished result that speaks for itself.

Most roofing contractors operate through layers of subcontractors, outside supervisors who never visit the site, and a sales model that optimizes for volume rather than quality. We built something different — a compressed, highly functional team where senior people are on your job site, not behind a desk.

We serve Orange, Seminole, Osceola, Lake, Polk, Volusia, and Brevard counties — the full Central Florida market — with the same direct accountability on every project regardless of size.

“Build it the way you'd want it built on your own home — every time, on every roof.”

Our Standard

👥
Fully Managed Crews

Every project is run by a crew that manages itself — each team has its own project management built in, so work stays on schedule, on spec, and accountable from start to finish.

⭐
Premium Materials Only

We don't offer a “budget line.” Every system we install uses manufacturer-grade materials from trusted brands — never a substitution or an undisclosed downgrade.

📄
Full Accountability

We pull permits, manage inspections, and hand you documentation at completion — including your warranty certificate, permit card, and material specifications.

📍
7-County Service Area

Orange, Seminole, Osceola, Lake, Polk, Volusia, and Brevard counties — the full Central Florida market covered by the same senior team.

The Team

Experienced People on Every Job

Our team is small by design. We don't scale by hiring volume and hoping for the best — we scale by building a core team of experienced craftsmen who take ownership of every project they touch.

Every crew member understands the Florida building environment: wind design requirements, tie-in specifications, deck inspection protocols, and the manufacturer installation details that determine whether a warranty claim is honored when it matters.

Navy shirts, white trucks, and the same faces on every job. That's accountability you can see.
