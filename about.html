Our Story

Who We Are

Built on a Simple Idea

Caliber Construction Group was built on a simple idea: hire experienced craftsmen, install the best materials, and deliver a finished result that speaks for itself.

Most roofing contractors operate through layers of subcontractors, project managers who never visit the site, and a sales model that optimizes for volume rather than quality. We built something different — a compressed, highly functional team where senior people are on your job site, not behind a desk.

We serve Orange, Seminole, Osceola, Lake, Polk, Volusia, and Brevard counties — the full Central Florida market — with the same direct accountability on every project regardless of size.

Our Standard

Senior On-Site Management

Every project has a senior team member on your job site — overseeing the crew, inspecting installation, and making sure every detail meets our standard.

The Team

Experienced People on Every Job

Our team is small by design. We don't scale by hiring volume and hoping for the best — we scale by building a core team of experienced craftsmen who take ownership of every project they touch.

Every crew member understands the Florida building environment: wind design requirements, tie-in specifications, deck inspection protocols, and the manufacturer installation details that determine whether a warranty claim is honored when it matters.

Navy shirts, white trucks, and the same faces on every job. That's accountability you can see.
