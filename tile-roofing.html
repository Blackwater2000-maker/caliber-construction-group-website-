Caliber Installation

Professional Tile Installation

Tile roofing requires more precision than any other system — correct underlayment, proper battening, precise mortar and foam adhesive application, and strict attention to uplift specifications. Caliber's installation crews are trained on Westlake Royal products and follow manufacturer installation guidelines on every job.

We verify structural capacity before every tile install. Concrete tile adds significant dead load — we assess your roof decking, framing, and span before a single tile is ordered. Every installation is permitted, inspected, and backed by our 5-year workmanship warranty on top of the manufacturer coverage.

  • Structural assessment included on every job
  • All permits pulled and managed by Caliber
  • Westlake Royal product-trained installation crews
  • 5-Year Caliber workmanship warranty
  • Manufacturer warranties from 50 years to Lifetime
  • FORTIFIED-eligible systems available
  • Cool Roof options to reduce energy costs
● 5-Year Workmanship + Manufacturer Coverage

Every Westlake Royal tile installation by Caliber is backed by our 5-year workmanship warranty covering flashing, mortar, underlayment, and fastening systems — stacked on top of the manufacturer's coverage. US Tile and DaVinci carry Lifetime Limited material warranties. Unified Steel carries a 50-Year Transferable warranty. Newpoint™ carries a Non-Prorated, Fully Transferable Lifetime warranty. All products are Class A fire-rated and hurricane wind-performance rated.
