Clay & Concrete Tile Roofing

Tile Roofing Done the Right Way

The Florida Standard

Built to Last 50+ Years

Clay barrel tiles — the classic terracotta orange-red, S-curve profile — have defined Florida architecture for over a century. Their thermal mass regulates interior temperatures, their density resists hurricane-force winds, and their fired-clay composition never fades or corrodes in Florida's salt air and intense UV environment.

Concrete flat tiles offer the same performance in a modern, low-profile aesthetic. The sand and beige finish and clean horizontal lines complement contemporary Florida architecture while delivering the weight and durability that Florida's building code demands for long-term performance.

Both systems are heavy — which is exactly the point. Dead weight resists wind uplift. We verify structural capacity before every tile installation and handle all permitting and inspections through completion.

  • Clay barrel tile — terracotta & custom color options
  • Concrete flat tile — sand, beige, and designer finishes
  • 50+ year expected service life
  • Hurricane wind-rated installation
  • Structural assessment included
  • Permits pulled and managed
  • Dedicated on-site manager on every job
